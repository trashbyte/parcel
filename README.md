## Parcel

*Note: Parcel is a work in progress. This page explains its goals and planned features.*

Parcel is a lightweight modpack management tool for Minecraft, designed for easy collaboration and rapid updates during the development of modpacks.

### Goals

* *Simple, lightweight, easy to use*

Parcel should be usable by anyone looking to create or collaborate on a modpack. It has a simple and user-friendly interface.

* *Painless updates*

Parcel is designed with development in mind, and offers robust support for adjusting and updating existing packs in-place.

* *Independent, portable, cross-platform*

Parcel isn't tied to any particular mod ecosystem, and supports downloading mods from CurseForge, Modrinth, GitHub, plain URLs and more. It uses a simple TOML modlist format, allowing modpack authors to distribute their pack as a single text file. Written in Python, Parcel is cross-platform and runs on Windows, MacOS, and Linux.
